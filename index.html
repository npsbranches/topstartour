Why Uzbekistan?

Uzbekistan is a large and majestic ship sailing on the waves of its hills. Immerse yourself in the fairy tale of the east with TopStarTour. Uzbekistan is a unique, colorful, hospitable, flourishing region of Central Asia. The riot of colors of the ancient East is closely intertwined with modernity. Fragrant oriental bazaars, unshakable architecture of the ancient East with its striking eyes minarets, domes in the turquoise which are played by sun glare. And the eastern countries will amaze you with their variety and taste. Do not describe in words the impressions that you get by visiting Uzbekistan
